Layout Options

Content centered, buildings on the sides — pick a direction for the zoom effect

A — Flanking Panels

Buildings split into two sticky side panels. Content centered in the middle column. On scroll, buildings zoom in slowly (scale 1.0 → 1.15) giving depth as you read through sections.

B — Radial Vignette

Buildings span the full background but are revealed only at the left/right edges with a dark radial vignette clearing the center. Scroll zoom (scale 1.0 → 1.2) pulls the buildings "toward you" from the sides.

C — Narrow Edge Strips

Buildings are narrow vertical strips at the far left and right edges, partially faded out. Content takes most of the width. Zoom effect uses a perspective transform (translateZ) so buildings feel like they're receding behind the content.

Click your preferred layout. You can also describe what feels closest to your vision in the terminal.
