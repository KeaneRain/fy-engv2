A — Scroll-Scrubbed Scale (Recommended)

The entire SVG canvas scales from 1.0 to 1.2 tied directly to scroll progress via GSAP ScrollTrigger scrub. The buildings grow continuously as you read — smooth, subtle depth. Works with your existing GSAP setup.

B — Section-Stepped Zoom

Buildings snap to a new scale at each section boundary (Hero → Services → Projects etc.), with a smooth tween between states. More dramatic jumps between sections, less continuous. Creates a chapter-like feel.

Both approaches keep your existing draw-on-scroll SVG animation. The zoom is layered on top.
